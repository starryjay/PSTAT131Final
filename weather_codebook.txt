Data measured at Santa Barbara Municipal Airport Weather Station (Global Historical Climatology Network Daily Station ID: USW00023190).

average_dew_point_o_f: Daily average dew point, degrees Fahrenheit
average_humidity: Daily average humidity, percentage
maximum_wind_speed_mph: Daily maximum wind speed, miles per hour
average_wind_speed_mph: Daily average wind speed, miles per hour
average_pressure_in_hg: Daily average pressure, inches of mercury
precipitation_in: Daily total precipitation, inches
